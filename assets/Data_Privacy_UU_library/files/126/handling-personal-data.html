          Handling personal data

          Check how to handle personal data before, during and after your research.

          The Algemene Verordening Gegevensbescherming external link (AVG), or the General Data Protection Regulation external link  (GDPR), came into force on May 25 2018. The GDPR requires you as a researcher to provide clarity and transparency to data subjects about how you handle their personal data. It demands that certain safeguards and security measures be put in place to protect the privacy of data subjects. For detailed information on Privacy and Research at the UMC Utrecht visit the UMCU intranet external link.

          Sharing of research data that relates to people can often be achieved using a combination of obtaining consent, pseudonymising data and regulating data access.
