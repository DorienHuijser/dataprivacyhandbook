Do you suspect a data breach?

Immediately report this to CERT! By sending an email to cert@uu.nl external link or (in urgent situations) by calling (030) 253 5959.

Training

We offer privacy training sessions online. At the moment, all planned sessions are in Dutch. We intend to schedule more sessions, also in English, at a later stage.

Are you handling personal data correctly?

At UU we use a large amount of personal data in our daily work. We want to handle this data carefully, because privacy matters. This often leads to questions: what is personal data? What should I pay attention to when handling personal data? Which privacy obligations do I have under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)? We will get you started here and tell you everything you need to know about the careful handling of personal data to safeguard the privacy of employees, students, researchers and others.
